8 Things You Didnt Know About Alishba Yousuf

➡ Alishba Yousuf Biography

Alishba Yousuf was born as Fariha Yousuf in the year September 16, 1985, in Karachi, Pakistan She is a Pakistani VJ, model, and actress is known for her astounding work in Pakistani television series and telefilms. She has appeared in supporting roles in various television series. She is the sister of VJ Palwasha Yousuf and actress Syra Yousuf. Alishba is known for her sweet roles portrayed in serials which in a literal way suits her.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Alishba Yousuf

1- Before fame

Alishba started her career as a VJ at Tv channel AAG since then she has appeared in many tv commercials of leading Pakistani brands.

2- Family and Fame

Alishba is the sister of famous VJ Palwasha Yousuf and actress Syra Yousuf these sisters have earned a name for being successful in the Pakistani tv series and other Pakistani brand channels.

3- Personal life

She married Rayyan Durrani who appeared in the MasterChef Pakistan in an Islamic Wedding Ceremony in Karachi on 12th February 2009. They have a daughter, name Illyana.

4- Supporting Roles

Alishba has appeared in several series such as Main Abdul Qadir Hoon, Takkay ki Ayegi Baraat, Meray Dard ko Jo Zuban Miley, Annie ki Ayegi Baraat, Sargoshi, and Tanhaiyan Naye Silsilay.

5- Work with brands

Alishba Yousuf has scored in working with Famous leading Brands like Mobilink, Fair & Lovely, and Peki.

6- Debut Serial

She made her first debut in the serial Chand Pe Dastak on HUM TV, and since then, she never looked back and did efforts to take a place in the industry.

7- Channels worked with

Alishba Yousuf has worked with HUM TV, Geo TV, and ARY Digital on various projects for which she got huge applause and appreciation.

8- Working with family

Alishba and her sister along with her Brother in Law appeared in Tanhaiyan Naye Silsilay on ARY Digital.
