8 Things You Didnt Know About Luke Benward

➡ Luke Benward Biography

Luke Aaron Benward, professionally known as Luke Benward, who is an American actor, known for the role of Bo, played in the Netflix film “Dumplin”. Also, he was first appeared in “How to eat fried worms” in which he played the role of Billy Forrester. There are more things to know about him and here we are presenting some unknown things you didn’t know about.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Luke Benward

His birth & family background

Luke Benward was born on 12th May 1995, Franklin, Tennessee, USA where he raised and grew up with parents. He was born to Kenda and Aaron Benward and they are blessed with three kids, two daughters and a son, Luke.

He is also a great singer

Luke’s skills are not confined to just acting, but he is known for his great singing skills as he is blessed with a melodious voice that has won millions of hearts.

Acting and modeling is in his blood

Luke’s mother is an actress, model and also known for an acting coach. While his grandpa, Jeoffrey Benward is Contemporary Christian music artist.

He started his acting career in 2002

Luke Benward started acting career with the role played as David Moore in the film titled “We Were Soldiers”, released in 2002.

His hobbies

Besides acting, he loves watching movies, playing games, reading books during his off-days.

Rose to fame

He became popular and earned success in 2006 when he worked in the movie named “How to eat fried worms”, in which he played the role of Billy Forrester for which he won a Young Artist Award for Best Young Ensemble in a Feature Film.

His television debut

Luke Benward made a debut on television in the show named “Family Affair” and appeared in its 1-2 episodes and played the role of Jody Davis, aired in 2002.

He appeared in various music videos

Luke Benward appeared in various music videos, such as; “Concrete Angel” 2002, “That’s Cool” 2004, “Everyday Hero” 2009, and other popular videos are remarkable.
