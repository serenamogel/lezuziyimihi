    Alizée Jacotey Facts

  • In November 2013, she became the winner of season 4 of Dance avec les stars (French version of Strictly Come Dancing) with her partner and future-husband Grégoire Lyonnet.
  • Alizée was listed as the 2nd ‘Sexiest Woman in the World’ by FHM Magazine (French edition) in 2010.
  • She is well-trained in dance forms like tap, jazz, flamenco, and Italian style ballet.
  • Alizée’s dance routine from the single, J’en Ai Marre inspired the female Night Elf dance in the video game, World of Warcraft.
  • She has 2 pet dogs – Jon Snow Lyonnet (Samoyed) and Galak (Chihuahua); both dogs have their individual Instagram accounts.
