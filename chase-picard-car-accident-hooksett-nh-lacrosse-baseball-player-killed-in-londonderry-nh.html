Chase Picard Obituary, Death – vThe Hooksett Flag Community suffered a devastating loss yesterday evening. The Picard Family and Chase have been an essential part of this league since its beginning ten years ago, and they need our help during this terrible time. A contribution to this cause would be much appreciated. Our thoughts are with Ashley, Jeremy, and the boys as they grapple with the unimaginable loss they have endured.

You can rely on the Hooksett Flag Community to help you! Chase was a kind, hardworking, and kind young guy who cared deeply for his parents and brothers. Athlete with exceptional talent. Baseball, basketball, and flag football were among his favorite sports. A significant portion of my evenings and weekends were spent attending sporting activities. Ashley has dedicated her entire life to caring for young children, as many of you are aware.

She has made a positive impact on many people’s lives and is always prepared to provide a helping hand to those in need. Jeremy is not only a well-known member of our community, but he also gives his time to coach his children in a number of sporting events. I frequently coach additional teams to ensure that young people in our community always have a team to play on. The death of a child is indescribable, and no parent should ever have to go through it.

We are all devastated by this awful loss and the premature death of a life. Please assist us in offering assistance to the Picard family during this terribly trying time. I’d like to thank everyone ahead of time.
