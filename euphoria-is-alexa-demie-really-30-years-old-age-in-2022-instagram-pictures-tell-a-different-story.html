Euphoria: Is Alexa Demie Really 30 Years Old? Age, Instagram Pictures Tell A Different Story

Alexa Demie, the actress of Euphoria, is 30 years old. 

Alexa Demi’s age has been the topic of controversy when a series of apparently dated photographs of her with celebrities like Azealia Banks, Khloé Kardashian, and Kim Kardashian appeared on social media.

People started to accuse her of lying about her actual age to the public. Some people criticized her, whereas some showed her support. Social media users began to create memes based on the controversy.

As season 2 of Euphoria has been released, netizens have dug in the previous discussion about Demie’s actual age.

Is Euphoria Actress Alexa Demie, Really 30 Years Old?

People claim that Euphoria actress Alexa Demie is 30 years old. Her age was “proven” in a July Twitter thread, which included a list of the class of 2008 from her high school. Thus, some people now believe she is in her late twenties or thirties.

In addition, even Wikipedia has removed her birth details. Instead, it has listed the information about her birthplace only. One user on Twitter previously reported that Wikipedia had listed her age as Dubious.

Some of her fans said that it does not matter how old she is as she looks pretty than most of the young actresses in Hollywood. At the same time, the topic inspired several internet users to turn it into a meme.

Despite all the rumors and speculations, the actress remained silent on the matter. She has not responded to the memes and all the gossip on the internet.

What Is Alexa Demie Age in 2022?

Alexa Demie is rumored to be in her 30s in 2022. However, people think she had lied about her real age in the media. Previously, she was said to be in her mid-20s.

In the series Euphoria as well. she plays the role of Maddy Perez, a 30 years old woman who is in an abusive relationship. Many followers think that her age is the same as her character in the series.

Besides all these discussions, the situation has pointed out one major issue of ageism in the industry. So it’s no surprise that the issue has made its way to the silver screen in a world where anti-aging commercials targeted at women are continually developing.

Many actors have shared heartbreaking experiences of being mistreated due to their age. Especially for actresses, looking young matters a lot but creates mental pressure simultaneously.

Alexa Demie Instagram Pictures Tell A Different Story About Her Age

Alexa Demie’s Instagram pictures tell a different story about her age. The photos she has shared look more like a shot captured in the 1990s. The quality of the photographs indicates a different story, but it is not difficult to manipulate images these days.

Therefore, we cannot confirm her age through the photos on her social media handle.
