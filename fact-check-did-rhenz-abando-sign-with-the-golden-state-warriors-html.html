FACT CHECK: Did Rhenz Abando Sign With The Golden State Warriors?

Rhenz Abando’s Global Basketball Stardom In the summer of 2023, Rhenz Abando became a basketball sensation worldwide. He wowed fans and experts with his incredible skills during the 2023 FIBA World Cup, earning recognition as a rising star in the sport.

The NBA Rumors and Golden State Warriors’ Interest Rumors swirled that Abando might join the NBA, particularly the Golden State Warriors. Fans envisioned him playing alongside Stephen Curry and Draymond Green, creating electrifying moments on the court.

Abando’s Current Journey in the KBL Abando’s performances in the Korean Basketball League (KBL) added to the excitement. Fans eagerly awaited news about his next career move.

Rhenz Abando Clears the Air Recently, Abando addressed the swirling rumors. He confirmed that he has not signed with the NBA’s Golden State Warriors and called the reports “fake news.”

Abando’s Stellar FIBA World Cup Display Despite Gilas Pilipinas’ challenging record in the 2023 FIBA World Cup, Abando’s outstanding skills were on full display, leading to speculation about his NBA future.

Abando’s Commitment to KBL Abando, a 25-year-old rising star, has been making waves in the KBL. His explosive dunks and exceptional leaping abilities have earned him recognition.

PBA’s Interest in Abando The Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) has expressed interest in including Abando in the Gilas Pilipinas lineup for the 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou, China.

PBA’s Efforts to Secure Abando Efforts are underway to secure Abando’s participation in the upcoming tournament. The PBA has reached out to the KBL for clearance.

B.League’s Likely Denial While the PBA also extended a request to the B.League, it is expected to be denied, as most Filipino imports in Japan are preparing for their season opener.

PBA’s Commitment to Gilas Pilipinas The PBA’s outreach to the Korean Basketball League highlights their dedication to forming a competitive Gilas Pilipinas squad for the prestigious international tournament.

AJ Edu Stays in the Philippines AJ Edu is the only player remaining in the Philippines, with others like Kiefer Ravena and Kai Sotto already joining their teams in Japan.

In 2023, Rhenz Abando took the basketball world by storm with his extraordinary talent. During the 2023 FIBA World Cup, he showcased his skills, even though Gilas Pilipinas faced a challenging 1-4 record. Fans and experts couldn’t help but wonder if Abando would make the jump to the NBA.

However, Abando recently put all the speculations to rest. He used Instagram to set the record straight, emphatically labeling the news of his NBA signing as “fake news.” This declaration made it clear that he has not inked a deal with the Golden State Warriors or any other NBA team.

At just 25 years old, Abando is already making a name for himself in the Korean Basketball League (KBL). His debut season with Anyang KGC saw him contribute significantly to his team’s championship victory. His high-flying dunks and exceptional leaping ability have earned him a reputation as one of the rising stars in basketball.

The Philippine Basketball Association (PBA) has taken a keen interest in Abando’s talents. In preparation for the 19th Asian Games in Hangzhou, China, the PBA has requested clearance from the KBL and Anyang JungKwanJang to include Abando in the Gilas Pilipinas lineup. While Abando has not officially commented on this request, it is evident that efforts are being made to secure his presence on the team.

The PBA has also reached out to the B.League, although it is anticipated that this request may be denied. Most Filipino imports in Japan are preparing for the B.League’s season opener on October 7.

As the 19th Asian Games draw nearer, the PBA’s outreach to the KBL underscores their commitment to assembling a competitive Gilas Pilipinas squad for the prestigious international tournament.

In the meantime, AJ Edu remains the sole player from the Philippines, with others like Kiefer Ravena, Kai Sotto, and Dwight Ramos having already joined their respective teams in Japan for the upcoming season campaigns.

Conclusion

Rhenz Abando’s journey in the world of basketball continues to captivate fans and enthusiasts alike. While the NBA rumors have been debunked, his future remains bright, and the Philippines eagerly awaits his next move on the court.
