Fair Girls Are Not The Most Beautiful- They Are UGLY Beneath Their Light Skin

Lots of people, women especially live with a somewhat assumed notion that to be beautiful, one has to be light skinned. One of the many reasons why bleaching is still a canker that has remained unsolved.

According to one Nigerian woman, light skinned women are not even as purported to be compared to the dark skinned ladies. She says when she goes out with her light skinned friends, men tend to look at them more.

“Yes, most fair girls are ugly beneath. They hide their ugliness with bleaching cream they used to bleach their skin. Funny enough, these days, once you are not fair men look at you as dirty.

“My statement is really for the men who have been fooled. Black girls like us are cleaner and prettier than those so-called fair girls. Please, don’t let their bleached skin deceive you,” Charity Nnaji.

“When I go out in the company of fair friends’ men tend to give them more attention. They simply cannot resist the sight of fair laps. But my friends who are fair know in their heart I’m more beautiful with my black skin.”
