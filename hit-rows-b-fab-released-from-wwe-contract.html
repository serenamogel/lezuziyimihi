Hit Rows B-Fab Released From WWE Contract

B-Fab was primed to make a huge splash on the WWE main roster as a member of Hit Row. The stable was just called up to the SmackDown brand as a whole during the 2021 WWE Draft, but now they are one member lighter.

WWE released several people from their contracts today. Those names included Scarlett, Ember Moon, and Franky Monet. B-Fab was on that list as well.

Sean Sapp has confirmed that the Hit Row member was released by the company today as part of their recent wave of cuts. This is a big shock for fans all around.

Fightful has confirmed Hit Row’s B-Fab has been released by WWE

Now fans are wondering if the future of Hit Row is up in the air. We will have to see how the dynamic of that faction might change as the weeks go by. B-Fab was green in the ring and learning. She still had a great presence on the microphone and a character to match.
