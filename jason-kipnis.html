Jason Kipnis was born in Northbrook, IL on April 3, 1987. MLB second baseman who began his career in 2011 with the Cleveland Indians. In his freshman year at the University of Kentucky, he batted .337, stealing 11 bases in 12 attempts. He transferred to Arizona State after two seasons with Kentucky.
