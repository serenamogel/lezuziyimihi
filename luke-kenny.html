Luke Kenny is a man who is blessed with some additional skills and known for his multiple skills that makes him stand out among all. He is a great director, producer, actor, VJ, and critic, hailing from India. He along with his parents moved to Mumbai when he was just 3 years old. His grandparents had Irish ancestry so he is a half Indian- Irish. While after completing his studies, he then started his career as a DJ and became the first male who appeared as VJ on Channel V. While he came into the spotlight after playing the role in the musical drama “Rock On” in 2008.

Career

Luke Kenny took the start of his career as a dancer and inspired by the late famed star Michael Jackson. Later, he then joined Arshad Warsi’s dance team. In 1991, he then joined the Rock Band “Greek” where he worked as a singer. From 1993 to 1995, he worked as a DJ. In 1995, he then appeared as VJ on the newly launched channel V. In 1997, he made his acting debut with a movie named “Bombay Boys” in which he worked alongside Naveen Andrews. He also served as head of music programming till 2008. He also worked as a director and producer and has worked on the movie named “13th Floor” starring Purab Kohli and Sandhya Mridul. Later, he made his major breakthrough “Rock On” in the year 2008. He has also been a great writer and wrote for various dailies including Hindustan Times, and other big newspapers. He also worked as a head of several music channels including 9XO, Rise of the Zombie, and others. Luke Kenny also made an appearance in the Netflix original movie named “Sacred Games”. Likewise, he has acted in several projects and also gained a huge recognition for the performances he had given.

Achievements

The multi-skilled, young star has achieved huge fame and recognition for his performances and amazing contribution to the field of music.
