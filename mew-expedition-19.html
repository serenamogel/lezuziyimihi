Mew (Japanese: ミュウ Mew) is a Psychic-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Expedition Base Set.

Card text (Expedition Base Set print)

Super Psywave
ネオサイコウェーブ
Choose 1 of your opponent's Pokémon. Count the number of Energy cards attached to that Pokémon. Put that many damage counters on the Pokémon.

Card text (EX Holon Phantoms print)

Super Psywave
ネオサイコウェーブ
Choose 1 of your opponent's Pokémon. Count the amount of Energy attached to that Pokémon. Put that many damage counters on the Pokémon.

e-Reader data

This card has three e-Card Series prints. The card ID of the Holofoil Expedition Base Set print is B-85. The card ID of the Non Holofoil Expedition Base Set print and the P Promotional print is B-85-#. On the Non Holofoil Expedition Base Set print and the P Promotional print, the short strip contains Pokédex information, a brief card summary, and information on the content of the long strip. The species, height, and weight are present on the Holofoil Expedition Base Set print.

Release information

This card was released in the English Expedition Base Set, originating from the Japanese Base Expansion Pack, with artwork by Hajime Kusajima. In Japan, it was reprinted as one of the P Promotional cards with different artwork by the same artist as part of a McDonald's promotion that ran between May 25 and June 16, 2002. It was later reprinted in the English EX Holon Phantoms expansion as a Holofoil Secret card with artwork by Ken Sugimori. This print originated as one of the Japanese PCG-P Promotional cards, and was available through a Meiji Chocolate campaign in June 2005. This print changes "number of Energy cards" in Super Psywave attack's effect to "amount of Energy", literally changing the effect from counting the cards to counting the Energy. A Non Holofoil version of the EX Holon Phantoms print was available in early DVD releases of Lucario and the Mystery of Mew.

Origin

Super Psywave is a variation of Psywave, a move in Pokémon games that Mew can learn in Generation I. This card's e-Reader Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Crystal.
