Michael Phelps | Career

He had a very successful and illustrious career which started at the age of 15 after competing in the 2000 Sydney Olympics.

However, the real success came from 2001 onwards when Phelps made a world record in the 200m butterfly event at World Aquatic Championships.

Likewise, in 2003, he won a total of four gold medals, two silver medals and broke five world records at that championship.

Above all, he won eight gold medals at the 2008 Beijing Olympics and broke the record of seven gold medals by Mark Splitz at a single Olympic.

Additionally, Phelps won four gold medals at the 2012 London Olympics followed by five more golds in the Rio Olympics.
