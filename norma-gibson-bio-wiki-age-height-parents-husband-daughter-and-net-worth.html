I am running errands at home.” Speaking about his ex-husband, Gibson is an American actor, singer, songwriter, author, television producer, and model.

He is best known for his role as Roman Pearce in the Fast & Furious franchise, as well as his music career. Gibson began his career as a model, appearing in commercials for Coca-Cola, Guess, and Tommy Hilfiger. He then signed with RCA Records and released his self-titled debut album in 1998. The album was a commercial success, spawning the hit singles “Sweet Lady” and “Nobody Else.”

Gibson made his film debut in 2000 with the romantic comedy Love Song. He then went on to star in the films Baby Boy (2001), 2 Fast 2 Furious (2003), Flight of the Phoenix (2004), and Waist Deep (2006). In 2007, he starred in the first Transformers film, which was a critical and commercial success.

He reprised his role in the sequels Transformers: Revenge of the Fallen (2009) and Transformers: Dark of the Moon (2011). Gibson has continued to act in both film and television. Some of his other famous credits include the films Ride Along 2 (2016), Legion (2010), and Ride Along (2014). He has also starred in the television series Moesha, The Game, and Black-ish.
