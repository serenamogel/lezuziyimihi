O.T. Genasis is an American rapper born on June 18, 1987 in Atlanta, GA. He has achieved success with his singles "CoCo," "Touchdown," and "Jackie Chan," with "CoCo" reaching #26 on the Billboard Hot 100.

About

Stylized as O.T. Genasis, this American Rapper has well known singles called "CoCo," "Touchdown"," and "Jackie Chan," the former of which peaked at #26 on the Billboard Hot 100.

Before Fame

He originally signed with G-Unit Records in 2011 before releasing his debut mixtape, Black Belt, the following year. He signed with Conglomerate Records later on.

Trivia

In 2014, he was featured on Busta Rhymes' mixtape Catastrophic 2.

Family Life

He was born Odis Flores. Though born in Atlanta, Georgia, he grew up in Long Beach, California.

Associated With

Alongside Kevin Gates, Loaded Lux, and G-Eazy, he performed at the 2014 BET Hip-Hop Awards.
