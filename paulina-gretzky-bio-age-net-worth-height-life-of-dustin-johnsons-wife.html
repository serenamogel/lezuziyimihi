Paulina Gretzky Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Life Of Dustin Johnsons Wife

Paulina Gretzky is a woman who has juggled many careers in her life. She has achieved fame by dabbling in movies, music and on the runway. In addition to that, the beautiful model is also known as the partner of the professional golfer, Dustin Johnson who was a former world number one. Having been born into the famous family of esteemed actress Janet Jones and retired hockey star, Wayne Gretzky, Paulina Gretzky has garnered worldwide recognition as one of the hottest celebutantes.

Regarding her professional careers, Gretzky has featured in quite a few movies including Grown Up 2. She has also been the cover girl of many widely-known magazines as she is pretty endowed with a super hot body despite being a mother of two kids. The elegant singer has done well for herself even though she is from a very wealthy family.

Paulina Gretzky Biography

The gorgeous model was born to Wayne Gretzky and Janet Jones on 19th December 1988 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Her father Wayne Gretzky is a former professional ice hockey player; the highest scorer in the history of National Hockey League as he had more goals and assists than any other player. Her mother, on the other hand, is a veteran Hollywood actress who has been active in the movie industry since 1982. She has starred in a vast number of movies and has featured alongside her daughter Paulina Gretzky in the movie Alpha Dog.

Aside from being an actress, Gretzky’s mother is a professional dancer and a model who has made the cover of a semi-nude pictorial edition of Playboy magazine. It’s no surprise that Paulina Gretzky is following the footsteps of her mother. The sensational singer who is the oldest child of her parents has four siblings, a sister who goes by the name of Emma and three brothers; Tristan, Ty and Trevor Gretzky. She is the granddaughter of Walter Gretzky. Paulina Gretzky is a graduate of Nightingale-Bamford school.

Life of Dustin Johnson’s Wife

Regarding her career, the brilliant actress became a celebrity by posting breath-taking photos on her social media handles. Following her prominence on social media, Paulina Gretzky landed her first modeling gig by appearing on the cover of Flare in 2005. As a result of her unequivocal beauty which uniquely adorned the cover of magazines, Gretzky landed more modeling works including featuring as the cover girl of Golf Digest.

Paulina Gretzky has done well for herself as a musician, having thrilled Hockey fans with her extraordinary performance that is worth remembering over and over again during the Hockey World Cup in 2004 held in Toronto, Canada where she rendered the Canadian National Anthem. Having kicked off her music career in 2003 at the Heritage Classic by performing the song, I Will Remember You, the superduper model has gone ahead to impress her fans by dropping a song titled Collecting Dust while on MTV’s reality series, The Real Orange County.

Gretzky has also carried out some works as an actress just like her mother. She has featured in a few movies such as Alpha Dog (2006) for which she was cast alongside her mother. She also made an appearance in Guns, Girls and Gambling (2012) where she played the character of The Deputy.

Paulina Gretzky’s Family Life

In 2009, Paulina Gretzky got to know Dustin the famous golfer through her parents. Being that both were in different relationships at the time of their meeting, the pair didn’t kick-start an affair but eventually started dating in 2012. In the following year, Dustin Johnson took the relationship to the next level by proposing to the elegant actress and since then, the couple has been together. Although Paulina Gretzky has often been referred to as Dustin Johnson’s wife, the two are yet to make it down the aisle.
