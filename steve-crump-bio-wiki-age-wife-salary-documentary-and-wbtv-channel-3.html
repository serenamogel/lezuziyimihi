Steve Crump WBTV, Channel 3 News

Crump’s career began in 1980 when he got a job as a DJ in Richmond, Kentucky. Crump joined WSAV-TV, an NBC-affiliate as an intern. 

Crump also worked for news stations in Orlando, Florida, Lexington, Kentucky, and Detroit, Michigan. Additionally, Steve worked as an independent producer for Charlotte’s WTVI, BET, and Charlotte’s PBS affiliate before coming to WBTV.
