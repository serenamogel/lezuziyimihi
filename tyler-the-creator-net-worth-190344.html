Tyler The Creator Net Worth

Tyler The Creator, born Wolf Haley, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and music video director. He is best known as the leader of the alternative rap group Odd Future, and in 2011 he won an MTV Video Music Award for Best New Artist. Tyler The Creator was born on March 6, 1991 in Ladera Heights, California.
