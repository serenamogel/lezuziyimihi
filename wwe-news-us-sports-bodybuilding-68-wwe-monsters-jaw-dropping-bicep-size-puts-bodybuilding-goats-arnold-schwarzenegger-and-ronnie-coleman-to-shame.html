Arnold Schwarzenegger is considered more than a bodybuilding legend; for some, he is a true idol. The seven-times Mr. Olympia left behind a remarkable career in the field of bodybuilding. Schwarzenegger started acting in films in Hollywood and soon became one of the biggest pioneers in the industry. The 1980s and 90s action movies saw Arnold Schwarzenegger as the ideal choice.

Apart from acting and bodybuilding, The Terminator star also has a WWE Hall of Fame to his name and a notable political career. He is also the former governor of the state of California, who boasts a good physique, with biceps of 22 to 24 inches when pumped.

Throughout his life, Schwarzenegger had one of the most wonderful physiques in the entire world. With huge pecs, biceps, quads, and traps, Schwarzenegger was a true bodybuilding icon. Similar to him, Hulk Hogan in WWE also boasted one of the biggest biceps.

In the current era, another superstar who could be a match for Schwarzenegger is The Monster Among Men, Braun Strowman. Recently on Twitter, a post analyzed a few statistics between Strowman and Otis. However, one shocking thing which came out of it and struck the fan’s eyes was Strowman’s biceps size.

Strowman flexed a massive biceps size of 25 inches, even bigger than Hulk Hogan. Even bodybuilding legend Ronnie Coleman fell short of Strowman’s biceps with just 23 inches. While Schwarzenegger is a seven-time Mr. Olympia, Coleman is an eight-time Mr. Olympia. However, Strowman is neither.

Who has bigger biceps between Arnold Schwarzenegger, Dwayne Johnson, and John Cena?

Dwayne Johnson started as a football player before joining WWE and then Hollywood. Similarly, John Cena also pursued bodybuilding before joining WWE, whereas Arnold Schwarzenegger was a professional prize-winning bodybuilder who joined the world of acting after having a successful career in it.

All these three legends possess wonderful physiques with which very few can compete. But still, a question arises: who among these three has a bigger biceps size?

When pumped and flexed, The Terminator actor has a biceps size between 22 to 24 inches. While Dwayne Johnson has a biceps size of 20 inches and John Cena has 19-inch biceps. Thus, clearly, Arnold Schwarzenegger has bigger biceps than both of them.
